About Me

I've always been drawn to marketing, business ventures, and problem solving. Having opted into the business world from highschool, I naturally stepped into a B.S. degree in Marketing from University of Idaho. After some post college exploration in a new city and a bit of world travel I began my career with Vacasa. Working in the channel department as a Lead Listing Specialist for this forward thinking vacation rental company paved my way to a new dream.

While managing partner relations and ensuring listing quality on all OTA's was a great experience. It was digging into all of the tech issues I could not stay away from.

This inevitably led to continuing education in support of this new found interest. I'm currently attending a full stack web development bootcamp at University of Denver.
